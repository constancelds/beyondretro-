A UNIQUE AND PERSONALIZED SERVICE

The idea of creating Beyond Retro came from an observation: modern women are independent and working bosses, they like to be elegant but no longer have time to shop. Beyond Retro's role is to meet the needs of its customers, thanks to the advice of stylists and the free fitting of clothes at home. Our expertise and exceptional stylists will find you the most beautiful pieces in your closet, vintage and second hand. A package of 10 pieces will be delivered to you, according to your taste in clothing. Renewing your wardrobe has never been easier. Beyond Retro simplifies your life, saves you time, finds you incredible pieces and helps you reduce your ecological footprint.
